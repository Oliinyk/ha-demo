Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 31, 2017

H&A Security Solutions, LLC (the “Company”) respects the privacy of visitors to our web site, www.hasecuritysolutions.com. While offering its website users the advantages and convenience of web-based technologies, the Company also strives to employ practices so that information collected about prospective affiliates and visitors is used conscientiously and appropriately. Before accessing or using any part of the www.hasecuritysolutions.com website, you should read carefully the following Statement in detail.

This Privacy Policy Statement outlines the use of personally identifiable information collected on the website. This privacy notice does not encompass other websites referenced by the Company in print, online, or in e-mail. Our website may contain links to enable you to visit other websites easily. Other websites you visit – including those linked from the Company website – may have their own privacy policies or no policy at all.

Generally, the information collected through the website is information used to help the Company better serve its customers, prospective affiliates and visitors. It is Company policy not to disclose your personal information to unaffiliated third parties except as required or permitted by law.

Visitors: Information is collected for Company marketing purposes about pages that visitors browse.

A cookie is used in website system administration to keep track of movement of an individual user from one screen to another.

The Company’s web server automatically collects information from your computer and navigation patterns when you visit its site, including your Internet Protocol (IP) address, the computer’s operating system, the type of browser you use, and the specific web pages visited during your connection. The Company may also track data such as the total number of visits to our website and the number of visitors to each page of its website. The Company may use this information, in aggregate form, for system maintenance and to better understand how its visitors use its website and services so that it can make them better. The Company may also share statistical or demographic information in aggregate form with third parties for marketing or research purposes. This aggregate data will not contain any information that personally identifies you. The information collected is used to improve the content of the Company’s web site, enhance the value of its products, and communicate with prospective affiliates and visitors. The Company, its leaders, and its affiliates have access to website visitor information for purposes of communicating with affiliates and furthering the mission of the organization. Any and all information provided, produced or collected on this website by prospective affiliates and visitors may be used by the Company for Company marketing purposes.

The Company’s website is intended for adults. The Company does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If the Company obtains knowledge that it has personally identifiable information about a child under 13 in retrievable form in its files, the Company will delete that information from our existing files.

The Company uses state-of-the-art technology to keep any information you provide as secure as possible and have reasonable security measures in place at its physical facilities and on its web server to protect against the loss, misuse, or alteration of information collected from prospective affiliates and visitors. Requests to review security information may be sent using the contact information below.

The Company also has in place privacy protection control contractual obligations with its vendors designed so that personal data is protected from unauthorized access or disclosure. All Company employees must abide by the Company’s Privacy Policy. Only authorized personnel are permitted to have access to personally identifiable data about website visitors, and that access is limited to what is reasonably needed to perform duties and responsibilities. Employees who violate our privacy policies are subject to disciplinary action, up to and including termination.

Periodically the Company may use collected information for new, unanticipated uses not previously disclosed in its privacy notice. When its information collection and dissemination practices change, such policy changes will be posted on www.hasecuritysolutions.com.
